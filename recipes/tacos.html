How to make Tacos

Description

This recipe will guide you in creating flavourful rich tasting tacos to enjoy with friends.

Ingrediants

  • Taco shells
  • Mince meat
  • Taco seasoning
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Firstly cook the mince meat and taco seasoning on medium heat.
  2. Next assemble to tacos. By placing the meat inside the taco shell and decorating with tomato, lettuce and cheese.
  3. Finally top off your taco's with sour cream and enjoy!
